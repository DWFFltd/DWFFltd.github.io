Consumer Testing Prototype



Instructions



In this test, you will be shown single pages from three purchase journeys. Read through, but if there is a form - do not fill it out. After reading through each purchase journey, proceed to the next step, where you will be asked questions about the content of each page and your impressions on its design.



Please time yourself. If you have been going for 25 minutes, please pause and take a 5 minute break before continuing. It is essential that you do this.
